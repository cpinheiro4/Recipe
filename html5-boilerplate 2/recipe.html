Straw and Grass (Paglia e Fieno)

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 1 pound Basic egg pasta (see recipe)
  • 1 pound Spinach pasta (see recipe)
  • 4 ounces Olive oil

Sauce

  • 8 ounces Bacon, diced
  • 3 each Garlic cloves, peeled, smashed whole
  • 6 ounces Onion or shallot, peeled, diced brunoise
  • 1 quart Heavy cream
  • 4 ounces Prosciutto, diced macédoine
  • 3 to 4 ounces Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Green peas, For garnish

Preperation

  1. Gather all the ingredients and equipment.
  2. Make pastas according to recipes.
  3. Blanch together, shock and oil and reserve.
  4. For the sauce: Render bacon with garlic. Bacon will be crispy and garlic golden in color. Discard garlic. Set bacon aside.
  5. Add onion or shallot and sauté.
  6. Add cream, prosciutto, and bacon and reduce until sauce coats a metal spoon.
  7. Add parmesan cheese and pasta and garnish with peas.
